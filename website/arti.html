AUSPICIOUS ARTI AT NARMADA RIVER

River Narmada is one of the sacred rivers in India.
Every day people from all over the city flock to do morning and evening pooja here.
In Jabalpur Narmada river is like life line.
At Uma Ghat (Gwarighat) Narmada river pujan is very special event which happens every evening in the shape of Maha Aarti, similar to Varanasi.
Narmada pujan and visit to different temples nearby gives a different meaning to the trip.
It is holy, sacred and historic place to visit.
