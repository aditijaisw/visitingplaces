NIDAN WATERFALL

Nidan waterfall is located in Katangi, Madhya Pradesh.
Katangi is a village about 30 kms from jabalpur city .
Pincode of Nidan Waterfall, Jabalpur is 470880 in India.
The falls are about 1 kilometre away from the main road and can be reached by walking.
It is enjoyable all year round but it looks best during the monsoon months (July to September).
After getting some basic information from a friend, we decided to explore. We started off on a cloudy Sunday afternoon around 1:30pm.
