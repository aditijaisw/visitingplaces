The dhuandhar fall is a waterfall in Jabalpur district in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.
It is in bhedaghat, India as viewed from east side.
To get one side to another, there is a cable car which takes around 60 Rs. for a return trip.
This ropeway gives a magnanimous view of this magnificent watherfalls.
