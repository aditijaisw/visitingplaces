MADAN MAHAL FORT

Madan Mahal is a suburban area of Jabalpur famous for the historical Durgavati fort.
The area also has a railway station named Madan Mahal.
It was the ancient era that consisted of beautiful palaces and forts.
Most of the forts of the early period were constructed in such a way that it could tolerate any climatic conditions, and many of them are still standing strong.
Madan Mahal Fort is one such fort that still has to hold its glamour and grandeur and take people to the past.
t is maintained by the Archaeological Department of India and tourists often visit this fort to learn and know about Madan Mahal Fort and the era bygone.
It was in 1116 when the Gond ruler named Raja Madan Shah constructed this fort.
This fort is located in the South-Western part of Jabalpur.
As we all know that Jabalpur has a hot and humid climate so it is always better to avoid visiting these places, especially during the summer months.
