GURUDWARA AT GWARIGHAT

Gurdwara Gwari Ghat Saheb is located at Gwarighat which is a famous stepped embankment (a ghat) alongside the river Narmada in the city of Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh, India.
It is a small town on the banks of the river Narmada near the city of Jabalpur.
It is also associated with Guru Nanak, the founder of Sikhism
Gurdwara Gwarighat is on the picturesque South bank of the river.
Gwarighat gurdware sahib perhaps holds the longest nishan sahib in asia.
The most famous Guruparab of HolaMahlla (Holi) and Birth anniversary of Guru Amardas Ji is observed and celebrated here with great enthusiasm.
